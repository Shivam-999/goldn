WHO ARE WE?


The covid crisis has been heart wrenching.
In the past year, we saw our biggest strength - our numbers - become our biggest weakness.
We saw heroes step up & rise amongst us. But we also saw villains with no souls.

We are a group of young people who are done feeling like sitting ducks, hoping others to solve our problems.
And we want to make ourselves useful. Do what’s in our power.
As individuals, we can do little.
But together, as INDIANS we can make miracles happen.

Join us in helping those who need our support, those who are still fighting &
those whose lives got ruined due to covid.

Our principles guide our mission





HOW DO WE WORK?



UNITY

We aim to bring folks of this country to come together as ONE and fight.
We believe sustainable work is local-led.
By partnering with reliable & duly verified local organizations and leader
We want to implement community owned projects and drives.
And make an impact that you can see, feel and be a part of.



ACCOUNTABILITY

Too many of us have lost too much to this pandemic.
And no one is ready to take the accountability.
We can’t bring back those blessed souls, but what we can do is save as many as we can.
We pledge to contribute a fraction of our daily income, starting from as low as 1 Rs./day.
Join us in our mission to make india immune.



TRANSPARENCY

We want to make the maximum impact. Make every rupee count.
We strive to prove the impact of your every single rupee.
100% transparency. An open book.
We provide you real time updates through
social media, photos, details & gps coordinates of every project & drive.
